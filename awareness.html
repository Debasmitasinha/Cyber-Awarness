Cyber Awareness

OTP Scam

Fraudsters call pretending to be bank officials and ask for OTP to steal money.

  • Never share OTP
  • Banks never ask OTP
  • Ignore urgent calls

UPI Fraud

Scammers send fake payment requests. Users think they are receiving money but actually send it.

  • Receiving money requires no approval
  • Check request carefully

Fake Calls

Fraudsters create panic by pretending to be bank officials or police.

  • Never share details
  • Call official numbers

Phishing

Fake links are used to steal login credentials.

  • Check URL spelling
  • Avoid unknown links

Job Scam

Fake job offers asking for registration fees.

  • No real job asks money
  • Verify company

Lottery Scam

Messages claim you won money without participating.

  • Ignore such messages
  • Do not pay fees

Social Media Scam

Hackers send fake links or impersonate friends to steal money or accounts.

  • Do not click unknown links
  • Verify identity before sending money

KYC Scam

Fraudsters ask to update KYC using fake links or apps.

  • Use only official apps
  • Never share personal details
